At the age of 17 I graduated high school and began to manage a band full time. I also wrote all of the songs for the band, and took up guitar and lead vocal duties. We managed to tour a few times and secure two contracts from some indie labels. It was a ton of fun and gave me some valuable work and life experience.

I also took several hours of private art classes a week all throughout middle school and high school which turned into some amazing opportunities to teach painting at several restaurants around the Orange County area for Wine and Paint nights.

In 2016, I started a comic book company named "Super Dope Comix" where I managed to write, pencil, ink, and letter my first comicbook. We took the comic and some merchandise I had also created to a few comic book conventions around Southern California and Nevada, it was an absolute blast and I learned a ton about what it takes to take something from concept to completion.

In the spring of 2017 I began a new adventure, learning the fine art of computer programming. Learning how to code has been, hands down, the most challenging thing I have ever done, and it has also proven to be one of the most rewarding. I look forward to learning as much as I possibly can and I hope to go far with these skills.
